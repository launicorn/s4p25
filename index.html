About this site

The new Aymara bourgeoisie are, in a way, practicing indigenous futurism through architecture. They are acknowledging the melting pot of influences from which they came — imperialist and otherwise — while designing and building for the Bolivia they want. Bolivia is in a constant state of reconciliation between its colonial past and the desire for a global future.
